History

Alnoor Special Children School (ANSCS) Okara Cantonment is functioning as a Non Profitable Insitution , all encompassing to explore the hidden potentials of special Childrenand transforming them to valuable member of society.It is a branch of Alnoor Children School Multan ,which was established in 2002 and now maintains a strength of 392 students.ANSCS Okara Branch is started initially with two classes in small campus with limited facilities in Jan 2014.The insitution with it's sacred mission intends to build and start classes for our special children in a better and purposefully build new campus at Defence Officers Housing Society (DOHS) Okara Cantonment.
