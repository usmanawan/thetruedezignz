Brief History About


iGotcha is a digital marketing and branding agency created to help entrepreneurs build their business online using social media. The CEO and founder, Sameela Bibi has many years of experience in social media marketing, attraction marketing, lead generation, branding and leadership development. She is a 3x award winning marketer and a leader in one of the largests attraction marketing systems in the world. She has dominated leadersboard with some of the big names in the industry including Ray Higdon, Dianne Hochman,Todd and Leah Getts.

  • She has built global organisations of over thousands using social media alone.
  • Sameela, is an author, a blogger and a young mother of 3.
  • She has created her own trainings, ebooks and courses which can help those who are new to the online world.







Her passion has always been to make a difference in the world and to serve those who are overwhelmed not knowing which direction to take. She struggled for many years and totally understands the frustration of others. Sameela, saw a need in the marketplace after spending countless hours each day over the phone teaching entrepreneurs how they could stand out online,

"it's a competitive industry" she says, "and it's not always as easy as it looks. You can buy tools, invest in coaches and courses but will end up going through analysis paralysis, you will feel burned out just trying to figure out how it's all done. Setting up landing pages, sale pages, websites, logos etc it's not an easy task."
